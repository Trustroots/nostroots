Hi there,

I'm Stephanie, a volunteer working on a new community app for Trustroots—and I'd love to invite you into the process early.

The short version:
We're building a new way for Trustroots members to connect through a shared map—chat, local events, meet ups, hitchhiking tips, whatever the community has to offer.

We're still early, which means your voice can directly shape what this becomes. I'm looking for people to chat with for 30–45 minutes between June 1–5. It'll be super informal—just a conversation to hear your thoughts, ideas, and feedback.

Up for the task? Book a time here:
https://calendly.com/trustroots-community/new-meeting

You can also explore the working prototype we have so far: https://nos.trustroots.org/ There's already a chat function live—feel free to jump in and share ideas using #iwantto (what you'd love to do or see on the map).

The slightly longer version:
This project is about more than just features. We're exploring how to build a version of Trustroots that stays free forever, moves toward data sovereignty, and strengthens the long-term resilience of our community.

If that resonates with you, I'd especially love to talk. You can read a bit more about the thinking here: https://ideas.trustroots.org/

Would really appreciate your input—this is something we're building with the community, not just for it.

Thanks so much,
Stephanie
